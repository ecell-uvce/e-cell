ABOUT US

E-Cell UVCE aims to enhance and nurture entrepreneurial skills among the students. It strives to be a well recognised centre of excellence for entrepreneurial development to create successful entrepreneurs. The main intention is to motivate and encourage students who have an inclination towards entrepreneurship and uplift them to follow their dreams. The Cell plans to provide mentorship, industry-academia interaction for further support, etc., keeping in mind the goal of producing successful startups and ventures from our premier institution into the world.

EVENTS

BULL MARKET

UVCE Bull Market is an online virtual stock investment, skill enhancement, online contest. It is a week-long contest to discover the next best stock investor among engineering students. It is an opportunity for all the young and competitive minds to learn the basics of trading and test their skills in stock market, and risk free too.

EUREKA

EUREKA is an event that helps foster upcoming entrepreneurs and their innovative ideas. It aims to explore and develop skills by allowing participants to showcase their thinking and marketing expertise. The event sails through exceptional ideas, to drafting a well-thought business model and pitching to a panel of investors.
